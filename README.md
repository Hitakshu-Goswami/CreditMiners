# CreditMiners

> **Transparent Credit Scoring & AI-Driven Micro-Investment Advisor for Underserved Users**

CreditMiners is an AI-powered FinTech platform developed as part of the **FinTech Hackathon**. The project addresses two critical financial inclusion challenges in India:

1. **Credit invisibility** among individuals who lack a formal credit history.
2. **Limited access to personalized investment guidance** for first-time and small-ticket investors.

Instead of relying solely on traditional credit bureau data, CreditMiners evaluates alternative financial behaviour using non-traditional digital signals such as mobile recharge patterns, utility bill payments, and e-commerce activity to estimate a user's creditworthiness.

The platform also includes a conversational AI-based risk assessment that helps users understand their investment profile and provides educational micro-investment recommendations tailored to their risk appetite.

>

# Problem Statement

Over **190 million adults in India** remain credit invisible because they lack a traditional credit history.

Traditional lending institutions depend heavily on credit bureau scores (such as CIBIL), making it difficult for:

- Students
- Freelancers
- Gig workers
- Small business owners
- First-time borrowers

to access financial products despite maintaining healthy financial habits.

Similarly, millions of first-time investors from Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities struggle with:

- Financial jargon
- Lack of personalized guidance
- Information asymmetry
- Fear of investing

These challenges create barriers to financial inclusion and wealth creation.

---

# Our Solution

CreditMiners bridges this gap through two integrated modules:

## 1. Alternative Credit Scoring

The system analyzes consented or synthetic alternative financial signals to estimate a user's credit likelihood.

Example signals include:

- Mobile recharge frequency
- Utility bill payment consistency
- E-commerce transaction behaviour
- Digital payment activity

The generated credit score is accompanied by:

- Explainable AI insights
- Top three influencing features
- Personalized score improvement recommendations

---

## 2. AI Micro-Investment Advisor

A conversational assessment consisting of 5–8 simple questions evaluates the user's:

- Financial goals
- Risk tolerance
- Investment horizon
- Savings habits

Based on the assessment, the system:

- Classifies the user's risk profile
- Suggests suitable investment categories
- Generates educational portfolio allocation
- Simulates projected portfolio growth over 1–5 years under multiple scenarios
