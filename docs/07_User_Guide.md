# CreditMiners User Guide

## Introduction

Welcome to **CreditMiners**, an AI-powered financial inclusion platform designed to help users understand their creditworthiness using alternative financial behaviour and receive educational micro-investment recommendations.

The application is intended for educational purposes and demonstrates how explainable AI can support transparent credit scoring and beginner-friendly investment guidance.

---

# Getting Started

## Prerequisites

To use the application, ensure that:

- The backend server is running.
- The frontend dashboard is accessible.
- Sample data or synthetic user profiles are available.

---

# Application Workflow

The complete user journey consists of the following steps:

```
Open Dashboard

↓

Select Sample User / Upload Data

↓

Generate Credit Score

↓

Review Score Explanation

↓

Complete Risk Assessment

↓

Receive Investment Recommendation

↓

View Growth Projection
```

---

# Step 1: Open the Dashboard

Launch the frontend application in your browser.

The dashboard serves as the central interface for interacting with all modules of CreditMiners.

---

# Step 2: Select or Upload Financial Data

Users can either:

- Choose a synthetic sample profile
- Upload consented sample financial information

Supported information includes:

- Mobile recharge activity
- Utility bill payment history
- Shopping behaviour
- Digital transaction activity

---

# Step 3: Generate Credit Score

After submitting financial data, click the **Generate Credit Score** button.

The application processes the data and predicts an educational credit-likelihood score.

---

# Step 4: Understand Your Score

Along with the score, the dashboard displays:

- Top three influencing features
- Feature importance
- Explainable AI insights
- Suggestions to improve creditworthiness

This allows users to understand why the score was generated.

---

# Step 5: Complete the Risk Assessment

Answer the conversational questionnaire consisting of approximately 5–8 questions.

Example questions include:

- What is your investment goal?
- How long do you plan to invest?
- How would you react to market fluctuations?
- What is your monthly investment budget?

---

# Step 6: View Investment Recommendation

Based on your responses, CreditMiners assigns a risk category:

- Low Risk
- Medium Risk
- High Risk

The platform then recommends suitable educational investment categories.

---

# Step 7: Review Growth Projection

The dashboard displays simulated portfolio growth under three scenarios:

- Conservative
- Moderate
- Optimistic

These projections are intended to help users understand potential long-term investment outcomes.

---

# Dashboard Features

The dashboard provides:

- Credit Score
- Explainable AI Insights
- Score Improvement Suggestions
- Risk Profile
- Investment Allocation
- Growth Projection Charts

---

# Educational Disclaimer

All credit scores, recommendations, and investment projections generated by CreditMiners are intended solely for educational and demonstration purposes.

They should not be interpreted as regulated financial advice.

---

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is my credit score an official CIBIL score?

No.

The generated score is an educational estimate based on alternative financial behaviour and is not affiliated with any credit bureau.

---

## Is my financial information stored?

The prototype uses synthetic or user-consented sample data for demonstration.

---

## Are the investment recommendations guaranteed?

No.

The recommendations are educational examples designed to demonstrate personalized financial guidance.
