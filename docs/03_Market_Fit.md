# Market Fit & Real-World Relevance

## Overview

Financial inclusion remains one of India's most significant economic and social challenges. While digital payment systems have transformed how people transact, access to credit and personalized financial guidance continues to be limited for millions of individuals.

CreditMiners addresses this gap by providing an AI-powered platform that combines alternative credit scoring with educational micro-investment guidance. The solution is designed to help individuals who are often excluded from traditional financial systems while encouraging responsible financial behaviour and investment awareness.

---

# Target Audience

The primary users of CreditMiners include individuals who have limited or no formal credit history but actively participate in the digital economy.

### Primary Users

- Students
- First-time borrowers
- Freelancers
- Gig workers
- Small business owners
- Self-employed professionals
- Users from Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities

### Secondary Users

The platform can also benefit:

- Banks
- NBFCs
- FinTech companies
- Microfinance institutions
- Financial literacy programs

---

# Existing Challenges

Traditional financial institutions rely heavily on historical borrowing records.

As a result:

- Responsible users without previous loans cannot build credit.
- Young professionals struggle to access financial products.
- Small investors often avoid investing due to lack of guidance.
- Financial products appear complicated for beginners.

These limitations reduce financial inclusion and slow wealth creation.

---

# Our Value Proposition

CreditMiners addresses these issues by:

- Evaluating alternative financial behaviour instead of only credit history.
- Providing transparent and explainable AI-generated credit scores.
- Recommending practical actions to improve financial health.
- Offering beginner-friendly investment recommendations.
- Promoting financial literacy through conversational interactions.
